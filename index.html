SERVER RULES

Read carefully. Ignorance is not an excuse.

1.1
Respect All Players - සියලුම ක්‍රීඩකයින්ට ගරු කරන්න
Ban
Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or toxicity of any kind will result in immediate permanent removal. Treat everyone with respect.
2.1
No Metagaming - Metagaming නැත
Ban
Using information obtained outside of your character's knowledge is strictly forbidden. This includes stream sniping, Discord calls during RP, or using OOC info IC.
2.2
No Powergaming - Powergaming නැත
Temp Ban
You must give other players a chance to react to your actions. Forcing outcomes without allowing counterplay ruins the experience for everyone.
1.2
Stay In Character - චරිතය තුළ රැඳී සිටින්න
Warn
Breaking character during active roleplay is not allowed. Use designated OOC channels for out-of-character communication only.
2.3
Fear RP Mandatory - බිය RP අනිවාර්යය
Temp Ban
Your character must react realistically to threats. If someone points a gun at you, act like your life is in danger. No hero complexes.
2.4
No Random Deathmatch - අහඹු මරණයක් නැත
Ban
Killing or attacking players without valid in-character reason is prohibited. Build up to conflict through RP, not violence.
3.1
No Exploits - සූරාකෑම් නැත
Ban
Using bugs, glitches, or unintended mechanics for advantage is forbidden. Report exploits to staff for rewards instead of abusing them.
3.2
No Third-Party Tools - තෙවන පාර්ශවීය මෙවලම් නැත
Perm Ban
Cheats, mods, injectors, or any external software that gives advantage results in immediate permanent ban with no appeal.
2.5
New Life Rule - නව ජීවන රීතිය
Warn
After respawning, you forget the events leading to your death. You cannot return to the scene or seek revenge for your previous life.
2.6
Combat Logging - සටන් ලොග් කිරීම
Ban
Disconnecting during or immediately after hostile RP to avoid consequences is prohibited. Wait 15 minutes after any combat before logging.
